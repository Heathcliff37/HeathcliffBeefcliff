With his original song, “I Pooped in the Grass”, featured on Tosh.0, Heathcliff earned the highest rated solo musical performance on national television. He has a diverse musical catalog in many genres where he plays harmonica, sings, raps, and occasionally performs using obscure forms of sound production. He is known for his witty lyrics and high energy. Whether it's a leather jacket or cow costume, you’ll almost always see Heathcliff wearing some sort of "beef"! Heathcliff loves the dichotomy of the serious-vs-silly look, which corresponds to the dichotomy of his serious and silly songs.

Heathcliff intends to use his platform to advocate for civil rights reform, which he is personally passionate about. Heathcliff hopes to be a role model promoting a drug and alcohol free lifestyle.

Heathcliff also boasts one of the generation’s most successful and diversely published acting and modeling portfolios, including a landmark commercial with LeBron James, among many other national TV appearances and large scale publications. Heathcliff also holds a bachelor’s degree in Marine Science from the University of Florida and actively participates in research and academia alongside his music career.
