About

During his freshman year of university, Heathcliff had an accident while using his Heelys on campus. After that incident, Heathcliff had been too traumatized to use them. Recently, Heathcliff resolved to conquer that trauma, and he wrote a song about it, “Heely Trauma”. The song uses a silly context to convey a serious message about conquering trauma of all kinds.
