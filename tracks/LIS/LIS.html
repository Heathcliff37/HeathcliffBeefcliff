About

“LIS” is an abbreviation meaning “laugh in silence.” Heathcliff created it ten years ago to replace “lol”. Heathcliff realized that people used “lol” even when they barely made a sound, so he decided to make and promote the more accurate “LIS”. It became very popular and can be seen all around social media along with offshoots such as “ROTFLIS.”
