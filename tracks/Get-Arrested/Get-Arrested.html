About

"Get Arrested" is intended as both a silly fun Tik-Tok routine and a tongue in cheek political civil rights commentary on American police practices, which seem overkill and extreme compared to practices in other countries. Heathcliff is bona-fide social justice warrior and tries to use his music to promote civil rights, as displayed in this song in particular.
