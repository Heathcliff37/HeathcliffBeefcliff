About

Many girls tend to bend over in their dances, hence the suggestion to "Lick the Floor".

Lick the Floor was intended to capture the trends of the platform "Tik-Tok". Heathcliff initially personally produced the song from scratch, but his producer returned it with huge edits and remixes, effectively evolving it into something far better than just a "Tik-Tok" song.
