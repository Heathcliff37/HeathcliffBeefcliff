 Annotations

 [DB1]Butter is made from milk.

 [DB2]This is meant to emphasize that she is fully clothed.

 [DB3]Heathcliff is adamantly opposed to tattoos, and this is a subtle suggestion of that.

 [DB4]This is meant to be humorous and Heathcliff is not actually Hindu. It is referencing the fact that many Hindu practitioners do not eat beef due to religious concerns.
