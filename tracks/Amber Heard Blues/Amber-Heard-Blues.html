About

"Amber Heard Blues" is a slow delta blues song about the comedic Amber Heard-Johnny Depp trial and situation.

Corroded shards of metal flew off the harmonica and got lodged in Heathcliff's throat while playing, hence the raspy and irreproducible tones displayed in the harmonica riffs.

Heathcliff was having enormous issues with his jaw (TMJ) at the time and only reluctantly recorded a harmonica part. Due to the jaw issue, he couldn’t play any complicated riffs, so he decided to play the same notes over and over while relying on the tone to make them all sound unique and interesting, and luckily the corroded metal shards mentioned above helped the approach to work. Overall, the riffs sound goofy bit impressive, matching the overall theme of the song. Heathcliff has said that the harmonica in this song sounds like a "dying goat".
