 [DB1]"lay a log" is another way of saying "squat down as if you are pooping." "log"="poop".

 

About

Frog hopping is a bluegrass ditty meant to be performed as a tik tok dance and riding back on the fun high energy style of his hit song “I Pooped in the Grass”.
