About

 "Chicken in My Boot" is a humorous hardcore trap song commenting on the trends of modern rap. It is the lead single on Heathcliff's debut album "Mad Beefcliff Disease."
"Chicken in My Boot" uses a lot of would-be innuendos to suggest clean interpretations for many of the same themes discussed in modern rap.
