Musician Bio


Heathcliff plays harmonica, sings, and raps. He also occasionally performs with other obscure instruments and forms of sound production. He is known for his witty lyrics and high energy. He writes all of his songs and enjoys displaying creative originality. He also enjoys occasionally parodying other songs with original hilarious lyrics.
Heathcliff is remarkably popular in eastern Europe and maintains a large audience in that region. In total, Heathcliff currently has over 10 million streams, and hopes to push that to 10 billion in the next decade!
Heathcliff primarily uses a Hohner Bb Marineband harmonica, but he enjoys various keys, brands, and models of ahrmoncias, including his Hohner Speical 20 and Hohner A marine band.
Heathcliff’s diverse musical style may be attributed to his diverse musical preferences. His musical influences range from Dean martin to Sonny Terry, to truly obscure eastern European folk music, among many others.
Whether it's a leather jacket or cow costume, you’ll almost always see Heathcliff wearing some sort of "beef"! Heathcliff loves the dichotomy of the serious-vs-silly look, which corresponds to his serious and silly songs. Heathcliff tries to capture a Hannah Montana -esque “best of both worlds” lifestyle and image.

Heathcliff also authored a globally successful self help book under another name. (He is currently keeping that identity secret in preparation for a grand reveal)
Heathcliff is currently working on advanced graduate degrees alongside his full time musical career.
Heathcliff is a devout traditional catholic and enjoys traditional Latin masses as well proper traditional eastern rite masses.
